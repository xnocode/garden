### Product Specification

#### Generic Product
- Developed for the general market
- One product serves many users
- Lower customization
- **Examples:** Microsoft Office, Facebook, Google Docs
#### Customized Product
- Developed for a specific client or organization
- High customization
- Usually developed for a single customer
- **Examples:** UCAM, Hospital Management System
