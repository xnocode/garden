# The Garden

Welcome. This is a digital garden — not a blog, not a portfolio, but a living collection of notes that I tend to over time. Some are polished essays; others are seedlings I'm still turning over in my head. Nothing here is "finished" in the way a published article is finished, and I think that's the point.

> [!info] What you'll find here
> Notes on **thinking**, **knowledge**, **note-taking**, and the slow craft of building a mind you actually trust. Start at [[Welcome to the Garden]] if you'd like a map, or wander into [[Digital Gardens]] to understand the metaphor.

I used to write exclusively in chronological streams — a blog where each post was a tidy conclusion, dated and forgotten. The garden is the opposite. Ideas here are interconnected by [[Wikilinks and Backlinks]], so a thought planted in one corner can quietly grow roots into another. A note from last spring might be revised tomorrow because something I read today changed my mind.

## How to wander

There's no right path. You can:

- Use **search** (⌘K) to follow a thread by keyword.
- Trace the **graph** to see how ideas connect.
- Read **backlinks** at the bottom of any note to find what references it.
- Follow **tags** to cluster notes by theme.

If a note feels incomplete, it probably is. That's the texture of a garden — things are always mid-growth. I'd rather show you the compost than pretend the beds are perfect.
